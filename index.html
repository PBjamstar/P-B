It is a legacy.

The Code

Laws of the Bloodline

✦
01
Respect & Behaviour
Show respect to all members, staff, allies, and enemies. No toxic behaviour, threats, or drama will be tolerated.
02
Crew Loyalty
PB is family. Disloyalty or double-clanning is not allowed under any circumstances.
03
Activity
Stay active in Discord and in-game. Inactivity for 14+ days without notice may result in removal.
04
Fair Gameplay
Follow all CNR server rules without exception. No cheating, exploiting, or unsportsmanlike conduct.
05
Voice & Comms
Use correct channels, avoid spam, and stay organised during operations and events.
06
Identity
Use PB tags correctly and represent the crew with pride at all times.
07
Media & Content
Do not share private crew content without permission. Protect the Phantom Bloodline image at all costs.
08
Age Requirement
You must meet the minimum age set by PB leadership. Maturity is non-negotiable.
09
Rank & Hierarchy
Respect the chain of command. Rank is earned through loyalty, conduct, and unwavering dedication.
10
Final Word
PB leadership has the final say on all matters. Terms may change. The Bloodline endures forever.
