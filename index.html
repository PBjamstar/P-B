The Crew

Who We Are

We are a respectful, elite Cops & Robbers crew operating across FiveM EU1. Built on loyalty, brotherhood, and the relentless pursuit of dominance — Phantom Bloodline is not just a crew.

It is a legacy.

"In the shadowed alleys of the CNR world, PB faced every assault and emerged stronger. Every attack became a legend."

Founded in 2024, the crew was forged from ambition. Rivals fell. Empires crumbled. The Bloodline stood — and it always will.

The Code

Laws of the Bloodline

✦
01
Respect & Behaviour
Show respect to all members, staff, allies, and enemies. No toxic behaviour, threats, or drama will be tolerated.
02
Crew Loyalty
PB is family. Disloyalty or double-clanning is not allowed under any circumstances.
03
Activity
Stay active in Discord and in-game. Inactivity for 14+ days without notice may result in removal.
04
Fair Gameplay
Follow all CNR server rules without exception. No cheating, exploiting, or unsportsmanlike conduct.
05
Voice & Comms
Use correct channels, avoid spam, and stay organised during operations and events.
06
Identity
Use PB tags correctly and represent the crew with pride at all times.
07
Media & Content
Do not share private crew content without permission. Protect the Phantom Bloodline image at all costs.
08
Age Requirement
You must meet the minimum age set by PB leadership. Maturity is non-negotiable.
09
Rank & Hierarchy
Respect the chain of command. Rank is earned through loyalty, conduct, and unwavering dedication.
10
Final Word
PB leadership has the final say on all matters. Terms may change. The Bloodline endures forever.
Hierarchy

The Chain of Command

✦
👑 Founder
The visionary who forged the crew from the fires of ambition. Eternal guardian of the Phantom Bloodline legacy.
🎩 Manager
The architect of operations. Runs the empire with precision and commands absolute trust from every member.
🔱 Boss
The iron fists beneath the velvet gloves. They speak for the top and don't waste words. Brutal, loyal, and feared.
🎯 Head of Recruitment
The gatekeeper of the Bloodline. Decides who is worthy to carry the PB name into the streets.
🩸 Bloodline Staff
The enforcers of order within the crew. They keep the peace, manage disputes, and protect the Bloodline from within.
⚔️ Senior Capo
Whispers travel through them. They guide Caporegimes and keep the crew's wheels turning in the backrooms.
🗡️ Caporegime
Sharp-dressed mentors with grit under their fingernails. They bring new blood into the fold and show them the way.
📋 Recruiter
They find the diamonds in the rough — scouts of the Bloodline seeking out the next generation of soldiers.
🎖️ Lieutenant
The boots on the cobblestones. They lead by action — in jobs, races, and showdowns. The streets are their domain.
💂 Soldier
They've earned their stripes. Trusted hands, loyal hearts, steady shots. First in and last out. The backbone of PB.
🤝 Associate
Fresh faces with fire in their eyes. Just stepped into the shadows — watch, learn, and earn your place.
⏳ Trial
Proving themselves under the watchful eye of the Bloodline. Every legend starts exactly here.
