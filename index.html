Who We Are

We are a respectful and elite Cops & Robbers crew operating on FiveM EU1. Built on loyalty, brotherhood, and dominance — Phantom Bloodline is not just a crew. It is a legacy.

Founded by jamstar, the crew was forged from ambition and shaped by every challenge thrown against us. Rivals fell. The Bloodline stood.

Whether you're a veteran of the streets or a rising soldier — if you wear the PB tag, you are family.

The Code

Laws of the Bloodline

01
Respect & Behaviour
Show respect to all crew members, staff, allies, and enemies. No toxic behaviour, threats, or drama.
02
Crew Loyalty
PB is family — disloyalty or double-clanning is not tolerated under any circumstances.
03
Activity
Stay active in Discord and in-game. Inactivity for 14+ days without notice may lead to removal.
04
Fair Gameplay
Follow all CNR server rules. No cheating, exploiting, or unsportsmanlike conduct.
05
Voice & Comms
Use correct channels, avoid spam, and stay organised during operations and events.
06
Identity
Use PB tags properly and represent the crew with pride at all times.
07
Media & Content
Don't share private crew content without permission. Protect the Phantom Bloodline image.
08
Age Requirement
You must meet the minimum age set by PB leadership. Maturity is non-negotiable.
09
Rank & Hierarchy
Respect the chain of command. Rank is earned through loyalty, conduct, and dedication.
10
Final Word
PB leadership has the final say on all matters. Terms may change. The Bloodline endures.
Hierarchy

The Chain of Command

👑 Founder
The visionary who forged the crew from the fires of ambition. Eternal guardian of the Phantom Bloodline legacy.
🎩 Manager
The architect of operations. Runs the empire with precision and commands absolute trust.
🔱 Boss
The iron fists beneath the velvet gloves. When there's trouble, they knock. They speak for the top and don't waste time.
🎯 Head of Recruitment
The gatekeeper of the Bloodline. Decides who is worthy to carry the PB name.
⚔️ Senior Capo
Whispers travel through them. They guide Caporegimes and keep the crew's wheels turning in the backrooms.
🗡️ Caporegime
They bring new blood into the fold. Sharp-dressed mentors with grit under their fingernails.
📋 Recruiter
They find the diamonds in the rough. Scouts of the Bloodline who seek out the next generation.
🎖️ Lieutenant
The boots on the cobblestones. They lead by action — whether it's a job, a race, or a showdown.
💂 Soldier
They've earned their stripes. Trusted hands, loyal hearts, and steady shots. The first in and the last out.
🤝 Associate
Fresh faces with fire in their eyes. Just stepped into the shadows — watch, learn, and earn your place.
⏳ Trial
Proving themselves under the watchful eye of the Bloodline. Every legend starts here.
